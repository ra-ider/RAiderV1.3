About Us

"We are an Interdisciplinary team consisting of people with a background in medicine and engineering. We aim to be India’s pioneer in aiding warriors Inflicted with Rheumatoid arthritis. Our journey started as a group project, but realising our common passion in developing aid and technologies that can provide a grass root solution for people in mass, we started working on devising concrete solutions. We are developing an algorithm to improve the lifestyle of Rheumatoid arthritis warrior with an end goal of having a one stop solution for it"
