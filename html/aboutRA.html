About Rheumatoid Arthritis and Solution



Rheumatoid arthritis is a chronic Inflammatory disorder that majorly affects the joint but can also involve other organ systems like the skin and lungs as well. It is an autoimmune process that is typically triggered by an Interaction between a genetic factor and the environment. Smoking is one of the most common causes. In the US around 1% of the population is affected which accounts for approximately 1.3 million people. It is usually more common in females than males. The prevalence in India ranges from 0.28-0.7%. The condition has a significant Impact on society over various parameters such as cost, disability, lost productivity and concurrently has a substantial Impact over the Mental Health of the person. According to various studies, depression is the most common comorbid condition associated with Rheumatoid arthritis which Ironically is often over looked and hence remains undiagnosed and left untreated.





WHAT IS THE SOLUTION?



We believe talking helps and so does a Harvard study, almost 80 years old, has proved that embracing community helps us live longer, and be happier. According to Robert Waldinger, director of the study, a psychiatrist at Massachusetts General Hospital and a professor of Psychiatry at Harvard Medical School, “ Taking care of your body is important, but tending to your relationships is a form of self-care too. That, I think, is the revelation.” Taking Inspiration from the study we decided to create a community network where various RA warriors from different geography and background can come to a common platform to share their story and form a community where everybody feels heard.
